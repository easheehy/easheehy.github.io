IdentifiBio Capstone Project

GitHub: Unavailble due to NDA

Synopsis

This project was done as a senior project working with a sponsor, IdentifiBio, to add a new part to their app which would make a new user login system for patients. This new system would allow patients to be able to schedule their drug testing, see any news relating to their health and have quick access to emergency contacts in case they are in need.

Team

As a team we have currently developed all the necessary software design documentation and UI design for the application and have started our work on programming the software in Xcode SWIFT and beginning the backend work simultaneously.

Individual

On an individual I have assisted with the documentation and am in charge of maintaining the servers where development is taking place as well as implementing the backend MySQL. Due to my experience with Xcode SWIFT I am helping lead the design in the programming aspects for the team.

INF 125: Video Game Project

GitHub: Will be available after project completion Dec 15th, 2017

Synopsis

A Bullet Hell game that is set in a post-apocalyptic future where darkness has taken over the world. The player controls an airship on a quest to secure a fallen star to help replenish the city's shield and keep the darkness at bay. Armed with a cannon and shields, the player must avoid damage or die.

Team

The team has currently developed the core mechanics of the game mainly with placeholders for art and basic music.

Individual

As an individual I am incharge of art, writing, and helping plan mechanics. At this time I have fully developed the story for the game and am currently working on all the pixel-art through using tutorials.
